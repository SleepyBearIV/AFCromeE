# Privacy Policy - AF Queue Monitor

**Last updated: October 29, 2025**

## Data Collection and Processing

AF Queue Monitor ("the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

### What the Extension Does:
- Monitors queue times on Arbetsförmedlingen's website only
- Displays exact waiting times in minutes instead of "lång kötid"
- Shows a floating indicator with current queue status
- All processing happens locally in your browser

### What the Extension Does NOT Do:
- ❌ Collect personal information
- ❌ Track user behavior or analytics
- ❌ Send data to external servers
- ❌ Store cookies or local data
- ❌ Access other websites or tabs

## Permissions Explanation

The extension requests minimal permissions:

- **Host permission for arbetsformedlingen.se**: Required to read and modify queue time display on Arbetsförmedlingen's contact page only
- **Web accessible resources**: Allows the extension to inject necessary scripts to intercept queue data

## Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security concerns. All queue time calculations happen locally in your browser and are not stored anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in extension updates with clear changelog information.
